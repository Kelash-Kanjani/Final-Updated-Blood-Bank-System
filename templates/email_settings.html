🔑 How to get a Gmail App Password (required)
  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/security
  2. Make sure 2-Step Verification is turned ON
  3. Search for "App Passwords" in the search bar at the top
  4. Click App Passwords → type any name (e.g. "BloodBank") → click Create
  5. Copy the 16-character password (e.g. abcd efgh ijkl mnop)
  6. Paste it in the App Password field below
⚠️ Use your Gmail address as the sender, and the App Password (not your regular Gmail password). Regular password will not work.
